# R07: AI Generalist Specialization

## Overview

A rising role in 2026 that focuses on outcomes rather than specific models. This course teaches how to orchestrate tools, agents, and data to deliver business ROI, bridging technical capabilities with business strategy.

## Prerequisites

- Beginner course track (B01-B10) completed
- Intermediate course track (I01-I03) completed
- Familiarity with multiple AI tools and frameworks
- Understanding of business processes and ROI concepts
- Proficiency in Python and rapid prototyping skills

## Target Role

AI Generalists are outcome-oriented practitioners who select and orchestrate the right combination of AI tools, agents, and data to solve business problems. Rather than specializing in a single model or framework, they maintain broad competence across the AI landscape and focus on delivering measurable business value. This role requires adaptability, cross-functional communication skills, and the ability to rapidly prototype and validate AI solutions.

## Key Tools & Technologies

- Multiple LLM APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, open-source models)
- Orchestration frameworks (LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI)
- Prototyping tools (Streamlit, Gradio, Jupyter)
- Business process automation platforms
- Project management and communication tools
- Analytics and reporting dashboards

## Learning Outcomes

- Measure and communicate the business ROI of AI initiatives using structured frameworks
- Select and orchestrate the right tools and agents for specific business problems without over-engineering
- Design and implement AI-powered workflow automations that integrate with existing business tools
- Evaluate and adopt new AI tools rapidly using structured evaluation frameworks
- Translate between technical AI concepts and business stakeholder language effectively
- Rapidly prototype AI solutions from business requirements to working demos for validation
