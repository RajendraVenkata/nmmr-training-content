# L02: Bridging Technical & Business Teams

## Overview

Develop the communication skills needed to translate complex technical AI concepts for business stakeholders and gather business requirements that can be mapped to AI solutions. This lesson covers communication frameworks, requirements gathering techniques, and strategies for maintaining alignment between technical implementation and business objectives.

## Topics Covered

- Translating technical AI concepts for business stakeholders
- Gathering business requirements for AI solutions
- Communication frameworks for cross-functional collaboration
- Managing stakeholder expectations about AI capabilities and timelines
- Creating alignment between technical implementation and business goals
- Handling pushback and skepticism about AI from non-technical teams

## Key Takeaways

- The ability to communicate across the technical-business divide is the single most valuable skill for an AI Generalist
- Business stakeholders care about outcomes (time saved, revenue gained, errors reduced), not technology details
- Requirements gathering should focus on the problem and current pain points, not on prescribed solutions
- Managing expectations requires honesty about both AI's capabilities and its limitations
- Consistent, jargon-free communication builds the trust needed for long-term AI adoption within organizations
