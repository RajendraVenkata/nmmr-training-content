# Business ROI from AI Systems

## Overview

Learn frameworks for measuring business outcomes from AI initiatives, conducting cost-benefit analysis, and aligning AI projects with strategic business goals for maximum ROI.

## Topics Covered

- Measuring business outcomes from AI initiatives
- ROI frameworks for AI projects
- Cost-benefit analysis for AI investments
- Aligning AI initiatives with business goals

## Key Takeaways

- AI Generalists focus on business outcomes, not specific models or tools
- ROI measurement is what justifies continued AI investment
- Successful AI projects start with clear business goal alignment, not technology selection
