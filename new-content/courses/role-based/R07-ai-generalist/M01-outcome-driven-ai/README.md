# M01: Outcome-Driven AI

## Overview

Focuses on delivering measurable business outcomes from AI systems rather than optimizing for technical metrics alone. This module covers ROI frameworks, practical tool and agent orchestration for business problems, and designing workflow automations that integrate with existing business operations.

## Prerequisites

- R07 course prerequisites (B01-B10, I01-I03 completed)
- Familiarity with LLM APIs and agent frameworks
- Understanding of business processes and automation concepts
- Proficiency in Python for rapid implementation

## Lessons

| Lesson | Name | Has Lab? | Key Topics |
|--------|------|----------|------------|
| L01 | Business ROI from AI Systems | No | ROI frameworks, cost-benefit analysis, aligning AI with business goals |
| L02 | Tool & Agent Orchestration for Business | No | Tool selection, orchestrating AI capabilities, avoiding over-engineering |
| L03 | Workflow Automation Patterns | Yes | Automation opportunities, AI-powered workflows, integration patterns, measuring impact |

## Key Tools

- LLM APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, open-source)
- LangChain / LangGraph for orchestration
- Business process automation tools
- Analytics and ROI tracking dashboards
- Integration platforms (Zapier, Make, custom APIs)

## Learning Outcomes

- Apply structured ROI frameworks to evaluate and prioritize AI initiatives
- Select the right combination of tools and agents for specific business problems
- Design AI-powered workflow automations that integrate seamlessly with existing business systems
- Measure the business impact of deployed AI automations with clear before/after metrics
