# L01: Business ROI from AI Systems

## Overview

Learn how to measure and communicate the business outcomes of AI initiatives using structured ROI frameworks. This lesson covers cost-benefit analysis for AI projects, aligning AI investments with business goals, and building the business case that justifies continued AI investment.

## Topics Covered

- Measuring business outcomes from AI systems
- ROI frameworks for AI initiatives
- Cost-benefit analysis for AI projects (compute, development, maintenance vs value delivered)
- Aligning AI initiatives with business goals and strategy
- Building compelling business cases for AI investment
- Common pitfalls in AI ROI measurement

## Key Takeaways

- AI ROI must be measured in business terms (revenue, cost savings, time saved), not technical metrics (accuracy, F1 score)
- Cost-benefit analysis should include all costs: development, compute, maintenance, training, and opportunity cost
- The best AI projects align directly with existing business priorities rather than seeking problems to solve
- ROI measurement should be planned before project kickoff, with baseline metrics established early
- Over-promising on ROI erodes trust; conservative estimates with room to exceed expectations build long-term support
