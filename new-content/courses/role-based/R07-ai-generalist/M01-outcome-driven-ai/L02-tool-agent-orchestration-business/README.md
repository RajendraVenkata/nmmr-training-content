# Tool & Agent Orchestration for Business

## Overview

Learn to select the right AI tools for business problems, orchestrate multiple AI capabilities together, and avoid over-engineering solutions when simpler approaches deliver the needed results.

## Topics Covered

- Selecting the right AI tools for specific business problems
- Orchestrating multiple AI capabilities for complex workflows
- Avoiding over-engineering: simplicity over complexity
- Practical tool evaluation frameworks

## Key Takeaways

- The AI Generalist's value is in choosing the right tool, not mastering every tool
- Orchestration across tools and agents delivers more business value than deep expertise in one
- The best solution is often the simplest one that meets the business requirement
