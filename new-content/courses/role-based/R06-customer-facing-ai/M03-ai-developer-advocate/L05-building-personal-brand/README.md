# L05: Building Your Personal Brand

## Overview

Learn how to establish yourself as a recognized subject matter expert in AI. This lesson covers documenting your learning journey, creating thought leadership content, building visibility within the AI community, and leveraging your personal brand to scale into senior roles.

## Topics Covered

- Being known as a subject matter expert in AI
- Documenting your learning journey publicly
- Creating thought leadership content that drives engagement
- Scaling to senior roles through visibility and influence
- Building a consistent presence across platforms (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, GitHub, blog)
- Networking strategically within the AI community

## Key Takeaways

- A personal brand is built through consistent, high-quality contributions over time, not through viral moments
- Documenting your learning journey is one of the most accessible and authentic forms of content creation
- Thought leadership requires having genuine opinions backed by experience; parroting trends is not enough
- Visibility and recognized expertise are increasingly important for career advancement into senior DevRel and leadership roles
- Authenticity resonates more than polish; share failures and lessons learned alongside successes
