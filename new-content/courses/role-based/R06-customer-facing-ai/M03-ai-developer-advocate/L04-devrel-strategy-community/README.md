# L04: DevRel Strategy & Community Building

## Overview

Learn how to design and execute a Developer Relations strategy that grows a platform's developer community. This lesson covers the awareness-activation-retention framework, planning workshops and office hours, creating launch playbooks, and gathering developer feedback to improve the product.

## Topics Covered

- Awareness-activation-retention framework for DevRel
- Designing workshops and office hours that attract and retain developers
- Launch playbooks for new features and products
- Growing platform user base through strategic community engagement
- Gathering and channeling developer feedback to product teams
- Measuring DevRel impact on developer adoption and satisfaction

## Key Takeaways

- The awareness-activation-retention funnel provides a structured framework for all DevRel activities
- Workshops and office hours are high-impact activities that build community and surface real developer needs
- Launch playbooks ensure consistent, high-quality messaging across every new release
- Developer feedback is one of the most valuable inputs for product teams; DevRel is the conduit
- DevRel impact should be measured with specific metrics (signups, activations, retention rates) tied to business outcomes
