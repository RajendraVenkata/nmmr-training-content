# L01: Deploying AI in Customer Workflows

## Overview

Learn how to work directly with customers to deploy AI into their existing workflows, navigating the challenges of real-world data, ambiguous requirements, and diverse technical environments. This lesson covers the end-to-end process from understanding customer needs to successful deployment.

## Topics Covered

- Working directly with customers to understand their workflows
- Deploying AI solutions into real production environments
- Understanding and adapting to messy real-world data
- Adapting AI solutions to fit diverse customer technical environments
- Managing customer expectations during deployment
- Iterating on deployed solutions based on user feedback

## Key Takeaways

- Customer environments are never as clean as development environments; expect the unexpected
- Understanding the customer's existing workflow is more important than the AI solution itself
- Successful deployment requires adapting the solution to the customer, not the other way around
- Building trust with customers requires transparency about what AI can and cannot do
- Iterative deployment with quick wins builds confidence and paves the way for larger AI initiatives
