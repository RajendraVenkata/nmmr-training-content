# L03: Customer Success Metrics

## Overview

Learn how to define, track, and report on the metrics that demonstrate AI value to customers. This lesson covers AI adoption measurement, ROI frameworks, usage analytics, and customer health scoring systems that predict churn and identify expansion opportunities.

## Topics Covered

- Defining and tracking AI adoption metrics
- ROI measurement frameworks for AI deployments
- Usage analytics for understanding customer engagement
- Customer health scoring methodologies
- Reporting and communicating AI value to stakeholders
- Leading vs lagging indicators for AI success

## Key Takeaways

- Metrics must be defined before deployment, not after; retroactive measurement is unreliable
- ROI frameworks for AI should include both quantitative (cost savings, revenue impact) and qualitative (user satisfaction, process improvement) dimensions
- Customer health scores that combine usage, performance, and engagement signals predict churn better than any single metric
- Regular reporting on AI value in business terms (not technical terms) maintains executive sponsorship
- Leading indicators (usage trends, feature adoption) are more actionable than lagging indicators (revenue impact, churn)
