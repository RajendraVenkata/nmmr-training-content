# L01: Post-Adoption Advisory

## Overview

Learn how to help customers scale their AI systems after the initial deployment phase, identifying optimization opportunities and building the long-term relationships that drive continued AI adoption and expansion within customer organizations.

## Topics Covered

- Helping customers scale AI systems after initial deployment
- Identifying optimization opportunities in live AI deployments
- Building long-term customer relationships through technical advisory
- Expansion planning: identifying new use cases within existing customers
- Managing customer expectations during scaling phases
- Conducting regular business reviews with technical stakeholders

## Key Takeaways

- The post-adoption phase is where most AI value is realized or lost; ongoing advisory is critical
- Proactively identifying optimization opportunities builds trust and demonstrates value
- Long-term customer relationships are built on consistent, reliable technical guidance
- Expansion within existing customers (new use cases, new teams) is often more valuable than new customer acquisition
- Regular cadence of business reviews and technical check-ins prevents issues from becoming crises
