# L02: Production AI Optimization (Latency & Cost)

## Overview

Develop the expertise to help customers optimize their production AI systems for latency and cost. This lesson covers practical strategies for performance tuning, cost reduction techniques, and setting up monitoring that keeps AI systems running efficiently over time.

## Topics Covered

- Helping customers optimize AI systems for latency
- Cost reduction strategies for production AI (model selection, caching, batching)
- Performance tuning guidance for inference pipelines
- Monitoring setup recommendations for production AI
- Identifying and eliminating performance bottlenecks
- Balancing quality, speed, and cost trade-offs

## Key Takeaways

- Latency optimization often yields the most immediate customer satisfaction improvements
- Cost reduction strategies should be approached systematically: model selection, caching, batching, and prompt optimization
- Monitoring is not optional; customers need visibility into their AI system's performance and costs
- Small changes (prompt length reduction, response caching) can yield large cost savings at scale
- Every optimization recommendation should be validated with data before and after implementation
