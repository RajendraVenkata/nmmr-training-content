# Transformer Architecture & Self-Attention

## Overview

Understand the Transformer architecture that powers all modern LLMs, with a focus on the self-attention mechanism that allows models to determine which parts of input are most relevant to each other.

## Topics Covered

- The self-attention mechanism (queries, keys, values)
- How transformers process sequences of text
- Positional encoding and sequence awareness
- Multi-head attention and its role in understanding context

## Key Takeaways

- The Transformer is the fundamental engine behind the modern AI revolution
- Self-attention allows models to understand which words are most relevant to each other
- Understanding the architecture is necessary for debugging and optimization in production
