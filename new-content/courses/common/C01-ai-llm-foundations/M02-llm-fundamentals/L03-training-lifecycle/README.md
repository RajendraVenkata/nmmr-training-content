# The Training Lifecycle

## Overview

Understand the complete lifecycle of how LLMs are trained, from pre-training on massive datasets through fine-tuning for specific tasks, and how distillation creates smaller, efficient models.

## Topics Covered

- Pre-training on internet-scale datasets
- Supervised fine-tuning with specialized, high-quality datasets
- Knowledge cutoffs and their implications
- Distillation: training smaller student models from larger teacher models

## Key Takeaways

- Pre-training gives models general knowledge; fine-tuning makes them excellent at specific tasks
- Knowledge cutoffs mean models cannot know about events after their training data ends
- Distillation enables smaller, faster, cheaper models that retain high performance
