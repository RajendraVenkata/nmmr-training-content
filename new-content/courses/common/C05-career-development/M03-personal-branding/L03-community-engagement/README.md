# Community Engagement & Meetups

## Overview

Build trust and expand your professional network through in-person meetups, conferences, open-source contributions, and active participation in developer communities.

## Topics Covered

- Attending in-person meetups and conferences
- Building trust with decision-makers through physical presence
- Contributing to open-source projects
- Participating in Discord/Slack communities and answering questions
- Identifying common pain points for product feedback

## Key Takeaways

- In-person presence builds trust faster than online interactions
- Answering questions in communities reveals common pain points and builds expertise
- Networking is not just about finding jobs - it's about building long-term professional relationships
