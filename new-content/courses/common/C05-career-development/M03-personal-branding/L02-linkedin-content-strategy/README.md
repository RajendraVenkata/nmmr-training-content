# LinkedIn & Content Strategy

## Overview

Optimize your LinkedIn presence and develop a content strategy that demonstrates AI expertise, shares problem-solving processes, and builds external credibility beyond what a resume can provide.

## Topics Covered

- LinkedIn profile optimization for AI professionals
- Sharing your problem-solving process publicly
- Building external credibility through consistent content
- Content cadence and consistency strategies

## Key Takeaways

- LinkedIn presence is career insurance in a competitive AI market
- Share what you are learning, not just what you have achieved
- Consistency matters more than perfection in content creation
