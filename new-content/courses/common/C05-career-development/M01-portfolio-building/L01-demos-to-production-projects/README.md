# From Demos to Production-Grade Projects

## Overview

Understand why hiring managers in 2026 dismiss basic chatbot tutorials and what they look for instead: system-level thinking, production reliability, and measurable outcomes.

## Topics Covered

- Why basic chatbot demos no longer impress hiring managers
- The shift from demos to system-level thinking
- Demonstrating production reliability in your portfolio
- Showing measurable outcomes and business impact

## Key Takeaways

- Hiring managers want to see you understand the full AI lifecycle, not just API calls
- Production-grade projects demonstrate reliability, evaluation, and monitoring
- Measurable outcomes (latency numbers, accuracy metrics) make your work tangible
