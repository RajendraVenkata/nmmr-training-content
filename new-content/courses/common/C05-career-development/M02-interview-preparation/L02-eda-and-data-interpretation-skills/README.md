# L02: EDA & Data Interpretation Skills

## Overview

This lesson develops your ability to perform exploratory data analysis with a business-first mindset, a skill that interviewers use to separate candidates who can run code from those who can extract actionable insights. You will learn to frame analysis around business metrics, detect data integrity issues, and interpret embedding visualizations.

## Topics Covered

- Business framing: identifying what metric matters before touching data
- Data integrity checks: detecting data leakage
- Data integrity checks: identifying and measuring bias in datasets
- Embedding visualization using UMAP for high-dimensional data
- Embedding visualization using PCA for dimensionality reduction
- Interpreting clusters and understanding what they reveal
- Communicating findings to non-technical stakeholders

## Key Takeaways

- Starting with "what business question are we answering" before any code separates strong candidates from average ones
- Data leakage is one of the most common and costly mistakes in AI projects, and interviewers test for your ability to detect it
- Bias in training data propagates into model behavior -- demonstrating awareness and mitigation strategies is essential
- UMAP and PCA are practical tools for understanding embedding spaces and are frequently discussed in AI interviews
- The ability to interpret clusters and explain them in business terms is more valuable than the ability to generate them
