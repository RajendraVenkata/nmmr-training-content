# The Storytelling Framework

## Overview

Master the storytelling framework for AI interviews: Problem -> Data -> Methods -> What Failed -> What Worked -> Business Impact, enabling you to explain technical decisions to both engineers and business stakeholders.

## Topics Covered

- The storytelling structure: Problem, Data, Methods, Failures, Wins, Impact
- Explaining technical decisions to engineers vs business stakeholders
- Communicating trade-offs between latency, cost, and quality
- Mock interview practice with AI tools

## Key Takeaways

- Communication often decides the final offer - technical skills alone aren't enough
- The storytelling framework works for any project discussion in any interview
- Including what failed shows maturity and iterative thinking
