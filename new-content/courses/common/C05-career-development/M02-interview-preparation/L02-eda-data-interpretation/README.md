# EDA & Data Interpretation Skills

## Overview

Master exploratory data analysis skills for AI interviews, including business framing, data integrity checks, and embedding visualization techniques like UMAP and PCA.

## Topics Covered

- Business framing: identifying the metric that actually matters
- Data integrity checks: detecting leakage and bias
- Embedding generation and visualization
- UMAP and PCA for interpreting clusters

## Key Takeaways

- Interviews often include dataset interpretation tasks - show your thinking process
- Always start with business framing before diving into technical analysis
- Familiarity with embedding visualization techniques demonstrates modern AI literacy
