# Trade-off Analysis & System Design

## Overview

Prepare for system design interviews by mastering the real-world trade-offs between latency, cost, and quality in AI systems, and practicing architecture decision justification.

## Topics Covered

- Latency vs cost vs quality trade-off analysis
- System design interview patterns for AI systems
- Architecture decision justification and defense
- Discussing real-world production constraints

## Key Takeaways

- AI system design interviews focus on trade-offs more than perfect solutions
- Be prepared to justify every architectural decision with concrete reasoning
- Understanding production constraints (latency, cost, reliability) separates senior from junior candidates
