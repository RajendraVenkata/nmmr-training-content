# L04: Strategic Relationship Building

## Overview

This lesson teaches you how to build professional relationships that extend beyond your immediate team during your first 90 days. You will learn how to conduct proactive one-on-ones, reach out to senior leaders authentically, build trust through consistent reliable work, and demonstrate the three traits managers value most: curiosity, coachability, and resourcefulness.

## Topics Covered

- Building relationships beyond your immediate team and why it matters
- Proactive one-on-ones with your manager: frequency, agenda, and follow-up
- Reaching out to senior leaders: how to do it without being presumptuous
- Building trust through "grunt work" and consistent follow-through
- Demonstrating curiosity: asking questions that show genuine interest in the business
- Demonstrating coachability: receiving and acting on feedback visibly
- Demonstrating resourcefulness: solving problems before escalating them
- The long-term career impact of relationships built in your first 90 days

## Key Takeaways

- Relationships built in the first 90 days often determine your trajectory for the next two to three years at a company
- Proactive one-on-ones with your manager build trust and ensure alignment -- do not wait for your manager to schedule them
- Senior leaders appreciate when new hires reach out with genuine curiosity about the business, not just self-promotion
- Volunteering for unglamorous but necessary work early on builds a reputation for reliability that opens doors to high-impact projects later
- Curiosity, coachability, and resourcefulness are the three traits that managers consistently cite as the strongest predictors of a new hire's long-term success
