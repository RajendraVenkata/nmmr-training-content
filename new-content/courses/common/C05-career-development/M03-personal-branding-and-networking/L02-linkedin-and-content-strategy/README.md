# L02: LinkedIn & Content Strategy

## Overview

This lesson provides a practical framework for optimizing your LinkedIn presence and developing a content strategy that positions you as a credible AI professional. You will learn how to share your problem-solving processes, demonstrate expertise through posts, and build external credibility that opens doors to interviews and collaborations.

## Topics Covered

- LinkedIn profile optimization for AI roles (headline, summary, experience, skills)
- Sharing your problem-solving process rather than just outcomes
- Content formats that demonstrate expertise: case studies, lessons learned, technical breakdowns
- Building external credibility through consistent, valuable posts
- Content cadence: how often to post and what to share
- Engaging with others' content to build relationships and visibility
- Avoiding common LinkedIn mistakes that undermine credibility

## Key Takeaways

- Your LinkedIn headline and summary are the first things recruiters see -- they should clearly state what you do and what value you bring
- Posts that walk through your problem-solving process are far more engaging and credible than announcements of achievements
- Consistency matters more than virality; posting valuable content weekly builds a stronger brand than occasional viral posts
- Engaging thoughtfully with others' content (not just liking, but adding insights) expands your network faster than cold outreach
- Your LinkedIn presence should complement your GitHub portfolio -- together, they tell a complete story of your capabilities
