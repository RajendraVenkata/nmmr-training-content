# M03: Personal Branding & Networking

## Overview

This module teaches you how to build a professional presence that attracts opportunities rather than chasing them. You will learn to build in public, craft a LinkedIn content strategy that demonstrates expertise, and engage authentically with AI communities both online and in person.

## Prerequisites

- Completion of M01 (Portfolio Building) or active AI projects to share
- A LinkedIn profile (even if minimal)
- Willingness to share work publicly and engage with peers

## Lessons

| Lesson | Name | Has Lab? | Key Topics |
|--------|------|----------|------------|
| L01 | Building in Public | No | Documenting your learning journey, sharing what you build, open-sourcing capstone projects, thought leadership content |
| L02 | LinkedIn & Content Strategy | No | LinkedIn presence optimization, sharing problem-solving processes, building external credibility, content that demonstrates expertise |
| L03 | Community Engagement & Meetups | No | In-person meetups and conferences, building trust with decision-makers, open-source contributions, Discord/Slack community participation |

## Key Tools

- LinkedIn for professional presence and content
- Twitter/X for AI community engagement
- GitHub for open-source contributions and visibility
- Discord and Slack for community participation
- Meetup platforms for in-person networking

## Learning Outcomes

- Build a consistent habit of sharing your learning process and project progress publicly
- Optimize your LinkedIn profile and develop a content cadence that demonstrates AI expertise
- Create thought leadership content that positions you as a knowledgeable practitioner in AI
- Engage authentically with AI communities through open-source contributions and forum participation
- Leverage in-person meetups and conferences to build trust and relationships with decision-makers
- Develop a personal brand that generates inbound opportunities from recruiters and hiring managers
