# L01: Building in Public

## Overview

This lesson introduces the practice of building in public -- sharing your learning journey, project progress, and technical insights as you work rather than waiting until everything is polished. You will learn why this approach accelerates career growth and how to do it effectively without feeling exposed or vulnerable.

## Topics Covered

- Why building in public accelerates career opportunities
- Documenting your learning journey: what to share and what to skip
- Sharing what you build: progress updates, architecture decisions, and lessons learned
- Open-sourcing capstone projects for maximum visibility
- Creating thought leadership content from your daily work
- Overcoming the fear of sharing imperfect or in-progress work
- Platforms and formats that work best for building in public

## Key Takeaways

- Building in public creates a compounding record of your growth that no resume can replicate
- Sharing your learning process -- including struggles and mistakes -- builds authenticity and attracts a genuine following
- Open-sourcing your capstone projects with clear documentation turns coursework into career assets
- Thought leadership does not require being an expert; it requires being consistently visible and genuinely curious
- The professionals who get the best opportunities are often those who are easiest to find and evaluate online
