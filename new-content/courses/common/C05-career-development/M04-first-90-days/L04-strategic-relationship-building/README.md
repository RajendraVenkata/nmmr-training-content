# Strategic Relationship Building

## Overview

Build relationships beyond your immediate team through proactive one-on-ones, senior leader outreach, and demonstrating the three qualities that define dependable new hires: curiosity, coachability, and resourcefulness.

## Topics Covered

- Building relationships beyond the immediate team
- Proactive one-on-ones with your manager (come with an agenda)
- Reaching out to senior leaders for 15-20 minute conversations
- Building trust through volunteering for unglamorous "grunt work"
- Demonstrating curiosity, coachability, and resourcefulness

## Key Takeaways

- Cross-team relationships are often the most important for long-term growth
- Being the person who "gets stuff done" builds a reputation that leads to bigger responsibilities
- By day 90, you should be known as curious, coachable, and resourceful
