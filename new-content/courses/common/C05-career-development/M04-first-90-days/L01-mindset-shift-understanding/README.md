# Mindset Shift & Understanding

## Overview

Learn why your first weeks should focus on understanding the environment rather than proving yourself, and how to ask the right questions to gather context that isn't written down.

## Topics Covered

- Understanding over proving yourself (Weeks 1-2)
- Asking the right questions to managers and peers
- Gathering unwritten context about team dynamics
- Learning team priorities and what success looks like

## Key Takeaways

- The first 90 days are critical because habits and relationships formed during this period compound
- Focus on understanding before trying to make an impact
- Ask specific questions about priorities, success metrics, and team dynamics
