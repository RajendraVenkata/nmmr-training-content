# Overcommunication & Visibility

## Overview

Learn why silence is dangerous early in your career and how to use daily update frameworks to keep your team and manager informed of your progress, blockers, and next steps.

## Topics Covered

- Why silence is dangerous: people assume the worst in absence of information
- Daily update framework: what you finished, what you're working on, where you're stuck
- Sharing progress and blockers proactively
- Being visible to your team and manager

## Key Takeaways

- Early in your career, overcommunication is always better than undercommunication
- A simple daily update prevents negative assumptions and builds trust
- Visibility is especially important in remote and hybrid work environments
